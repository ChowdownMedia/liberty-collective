Host Your Next Event at Liberty Collective

No matter the size of your gathering, Liberty Collective offers the perfect setting for your event. From intimate celebrations to large-scale parties, we provide versatile spaces tailored to your needs.

For more information or to book your event, email us at events@liberty-collective.com

Event Spaces

  • Private Event Rooms: Two exclusive indoor rooms for a more personalized experience.
  • Top Mezzanine: A spacious area that can be divided to suit smaller or larger groups.
  • Second-Floor Outdoor Deck: Enjoy scenic views and an open-air atmosphere, with year-round versatility.
  • First-Floor Outdoor Patios: Two patios perfect for relaxed gatherings and casual dining.
  • Volleyball Courts: Host a friendly competition or team-building activity on our five multi-functional sand courts.
  • Golf Simulator Bays: Four cutting-edge bays for an unforgettable golf experience.

Let Liberty Collective make your event memorable with our unique venues and exceptional service.
