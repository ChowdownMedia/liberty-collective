Liberty Collective Mug Club

Join the exclusive Liberty Collective Mug Club — where community, craft, and great pours come together. Membership is limited to just 200 members, making it one of the most sought-after clubs around. Each member receives a custom, numbered mug, crafted just for you and renewed annually to mark your continued membership in our collective. As a Mug Club member, you'll enjoy:

  • 10% off the Collective Pour Wall every time you visit
  • A $50 gift card to use in-house
  • Your own exclusive, numbered mug ready for your next pour
  • A Platinum VIP Member card to be used at every visit!

Sip in style, enjoy the perks, and be part of the community that makes Liberty Collective more than just a place to drink — it's a place to belong. Limited to 200 members. Once we're full, the waitlist begins — don't miss your spot!

Collective Uncorked

Welcome to Collective Uncorked, Liberty Collective's exclusive monthly wine club for curious palates and true enthusiasts. Each month, members enjoy:

  • Two hand-selected bottles – one red and one white – chosen from Cutting-Edge boutique distributors and not found at common retailers.
  • Two complimentary tasting experiences each month to explore new wines and connect with fellow members.
  • 10% off all premium wine pours at Caladesi Island Bar every Thursday through Sunday.

Discover unique wines, deepen your knowledge, and sip something new every month with Collective Uncorked.
